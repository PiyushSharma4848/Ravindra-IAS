How long does it take to crack UPSC CSE? +
Most aspirants take 2–3 years of dedicated preparation. However, with structured coaching, focused effort, and the right mentorship, many Ravindra IAS students have cleared the exam in their first or second attempt within 1–2 years.
Do you offer hostel/accommodation facilities? +
Yes! We have tie-ups with verified PG accommodations within walking distance of our Delhi and Bengaluru centres. Hostel details are shared upon enrolment.
Are EMI or scholarship options available? +
We offer 0% interest EMI plans for up to 12 months for all programs above ₹50,000. Additionally, merit scholarships of 20–50% are available for students who qualify through our scholarship test held every March.
Can I switch from online to classroom batch? +
Absolutely. Students can switch from online to classroom (subject to seat availability) by paying the fee difference. We recommend this before the Mains phase for in-person answer writing practice.
What optional subjects do you cover? +
We offer coaching for 12 optional subjects including Public Administration, Geography, History, Political Science, Sociology, Anthropology, PSIR, Mathematics, Economics, Hindi Literature, Law, and Philosophy.
How is the mock interview structured? +
Mock panels are conducted by retired IAS/IPS officers and simulate the actual UPSC Personality Test. Each session is recorded, reviewed, and followed by a detailed written feedback report.
